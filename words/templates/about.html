About

Vocabulary is a key factor in the development of a childs reading and writing skills. Vocabulary increases knowledge, reading comprehension, a person's ability to communicate, the probability of academic and career success, life skills, and enhances critical thinking skills. I homeschool my children and my son is not a fan of flashcards. I created this app because he enjoys looking at things on electronic devices, and we have more success on his spelling tests after he reviews the words online.
